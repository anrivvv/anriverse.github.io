Number Systems

What are Number Systems?

  • A numeral system (or system of numeration) is a writing system for expressing numbers;
    it is a mathematical notation for consistently representing numbers in a set using digit or other
    symbols.
  • Number systems help in representing the numbers in a small symbol set.
  • Number systems are systems in mathematics that are used to express numbers in various
    forms and are understood by computers.
  • Number Systems in Computer Science refer to the numeric base systems used for performing
    computations, storing and representing data.




What is the importance of Number Systems?

  • It helps us keep count of things around us.
  • Number systems help in representing the numbers in a small symbol set.
  • Used for computation in the banking sector.
  • Helps us in encrypting data, avoiding hacking and misuse of data.
  • Allows easy conversion of numbers for technical purposes.
  • It should be noted that every fiber of data gets stored in the computer as
    a number.
